## Full Description (4000 chars max)

🌪️ **Windy Pro** — the world's most powerful voice-to-text and speech translation app.

**Record. Transcribe. Translate. Share.**

Windy Pro combines cutting-edge speech recognition with real-time translation in one beautiful interface. Whether you're traveling abroad, conducting business across languages, or learning a new language — Windy Pro has you covered.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

🎤 **PRESS & HOLD TO TRANSLATE**
Simply press and hold the mic button, speak in any supported language, and get instant translations. It's that simple.

🌍 **15+ LANGUAGES**
Translate between English, Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, Italian, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Arabic, Hindi, Russian, Turkish, Vietnamese, and Dutch — with more coming soon.

🗣️ **CONVERSATION MODE**
Two-person translation in real time. Split-screen mode lets both speakers see translations simultaneously — perfect for face-to-face conversations across languages.

📷 **CAMERA OCR TRANSLATION**
Point your camera at any text — signs, menus, documents — and get instant translations overlaid on the image.

🔊 **TEXT-TO-SPEECH**
Hear translations spoken aloud in native accents. Adjust speech rate and choose from multiple voices.
