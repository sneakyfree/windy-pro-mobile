## Description
Windy Word turns your voice into text — instantly, privately, and beautifully.

Record meetings, lectures, conversations, and ideas with one tap. Get live transcripts powered by on-device AI. Translate between 15 languages with real-time speech translation. Point your camera at signs, menus, and documents for instant OCR translation.

KEY FEATURES:
• One-tap recording with live waveform visualization
• On-device speech-to-text (no data leaves your phone)
• Real-time translation (15 languages)
• Camera OCR — point, scan, translate
• Conversation mode — alternating speakers, dual transcript
• Cloud sync with offline fallback
• Quality scoring for every recording
• Export as text, markdown, SRT subtitles, or CSV
• VoiceOver accessible throughout
• Dark mode native design
• One sign-in across Windy Word, Chat, Mail, Cloud, and Fly
• See your agent's trust level (Eternitas integrity band) at a glance

PLANS:
• Free — 5-minute recordings, English only
• Pro ($49 one-time) — 30-minute recordings, all languages, all engines, cloud sync
• Translate ($79 one-time) — Live translation, conversation mode
• Translate Pro ($149 one-time) — Offline translation, 99 language pairs, medical/legal glossaries

No subscriptions. No recurring charges. Pay once, own forever.
